01 · The Shift

AI is the new search engine.
We are not yet in it.

When a potential client asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overview "which forex broker should I trust?" — ZERO Markets is not being recommended. This document outlines how we change that.

GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) is the discipline of ensuring AI models can find, trust, and cite your brand. Our May 2025 tracking round (48 test questions across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) shows ZERO already achieves 89% P0 citation rate on brand-direct queries — a strong baseline. The remaining challenge is threefold: converting neutral AI sentiment to positive, closing 11 P2 specialist search gaps, and eliminating negative third-party domain exposure that AI models are actively citing alongside our brand.

89%
P0 brand citation rate
When users search ZERO by name, AI models cite us reliably. This is a strong foundation — but brand-direct queries are not where new clients come from. Specialist searches are the gap.
11
P2 specialist search gaps
Queries like "best low spread broker", "best broker for scalping", "most trusted forex broker" return zero ZERO citations across all three AI platforms. These are high-intent acquisition queries.
7×
WikiFX cited per test
wikifx.com — which contains negative Korean client reviews — is cited more often than our own website. AI models surface this content alongside ZERO answers, suppressing conversion intent.
GEO Whitepaper · Key Finding
"A single content block authored by an expert, containing 10 core insights, carries far more weight in 2026 AI algorithms than 10,000 software-generated articles."
— 《GEO白皮书：AI搜索时代的品牌增长新范式》· Chapter 6.4
ZERO's implication: We do not need volume — we need depth. Our multi-jurisdiction regulatory expertise, trading infrastructure knowledge, and market-specific compliance insight are exactly the kind of scarce, expert-authored content blocks AI models will cite. This is a content strategy we can execute without a large team.

04 · Execution Plan

Four phases to GEO
market leadership

Each phase builds on the last. Technical foundation first — content investment before the infrastructure is ready is wasted spend.

01
2026 Q3 · Weeks 1–6
Foundation
"Make us findable"
Goal: Ensure every major AI model can crawl, parse, and trust ZERO's core pages. No content investment before this is in place.
  • Deploy llms.txt at root domain — declare core pages AI crawlers may cite 30 min
  • Update robots.txt to explicitly permit GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot Dev
  • Deploy Schema.org: Organization + FinancialProduct + FAQPage + BreadcrumbList across all key pages Dev · 2 weeks
  • Begin migration plan for blog subdomain → main domain (301 redirect strategy) Dev · Arch
  • Set up AI brand monitoring dashboard: weekly checks across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini for ZERO citations Marketing
02
2026 Q4 · Weeks 7–14
Authority
"Earn the citation"
Goal: Give AI models a reason to cite ZERO over competitors. Convert our regulatory credentials into structured, AI-scrapable expert content.
  • Rewrite core landing pages in inverted pyramid structure: regulatory proof + key stats in first 60 words Content
  • Produce 3 regulatory deep-dives per market: AU (ASIC client protections), NZ (FMA compliance), KR (local trading law) Content · Compliance
  • Build a 50-question FAQ knowledge base: each answer ≤150 words, structured for AI extraction Content
  • Add author/analyst pages with credentials, regulatory expertise, and bios — strengthens E-E-A-T Expert dimension Content
  • Secure citations on Wikipedia, Investopedia, Forex Peace Army, Trustpilot — primary sources AI models trust Outreach
03
2027 Q1 · Weeks 15–24
Expansion
"Own multilingual AI"
Goal: Capture AI answer space in Korean and Portuguese markets where AI search adoption is accelerating and English-first competitors have no presence.
  • Korean GEO content: 10 articles targeting Naver AI Search and HyperCLOVA scenarios — local regulatory FAQ, trading education KR Team
  • Portuguese/Spanish GEO: Brazil PIX payment explainers + local forex regulation FAQ for BR, MX, CO LATAM Team
  • Standardise hreflang tags across all 5 language versions so AI crawlers attribute content correctly Dev
  • Co-publish content with local analysts and KOLs to gain citations on high-authority regional domains Growth
  • Establish AI content compliance process: Compliance review → localisation → GEO optimisation → publish Process
04
2027 Q2–Q4
Moat
"Build the defensible asset"
Goal: Convert GEO capability into a durable competitive moat — a knowledge infrastructure that is expensive for competitors to replicate.
  • Launch ZERO FX Market Weekly as an API/RSS feed — AI tools can directly subscribe to our market data Data Product
  • Build ZERO FX Encyclopedia: 500 core trading terms, each with compliance annotations across 4 jurisdictions Content
  • Activate defensive AI SOP: 14-day response process for incorrect or negative brand information in AI outputs Marketing
  • Establish quarterly GEO audit: AI citation rate, competitive share of voice, content gap analysis Analytics
  • Explore AI agent integrations: Perplexity Spaces, GPT Actions, Claude Projects — direct brand placement Frontier
